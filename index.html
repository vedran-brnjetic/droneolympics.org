Drone Olympics 2019

We are calling the whole drone & UAV industry to join in a new kind of drone innovation event in Helsinki, Espoo and Vihti, Finland in 2.-5. September 2019

Read more about the challenges Become a partner
Made possible by Ultrahack

Let's make the most out of drones!

Take a part in challenging tasks and innovate new services & ways to use various technologies for e.g. in farming, forestry, rescue services, logistics, traffic and maintenance.

FLIGHT MISSION: Toxic Waste Problem

Map out the number and location of waste barrels, and identify any markings on the containers to assess their contents. Document the case and, if possible, take a sample of the vessel or the water.

Read more & join the challenge

FLIGHT MISSION: Building Care

Design an optimized flight path and estimate the rooftop maintenance need for given properties. Estimate the overall condition of the roof and walls, check if there are cracks in the element joints or any structural defects.

Read more & join the challenge

FLIGHT MISSION: Emergency Response

Provide information concerning an occurred accident for the Police and Rescue authorities. Authorities will give a briefing of mission onsite before conducting drone operation.

Read more & join the challenge

FLIGHT MISSION: Count the Grass

Estimate the total amount of grass crop harvest and map the crop variation. Competitors receive harvest information from a few samples.

Read more & join the challenge

HACKING MISSION: Flowering Forest

Count the number of purple spruce flowers and assess the condition of individual trees on one hectare testing area within a commercial seed orchard.

Read more & join the challenge

EXPERIMENTAL MISSION: Fly High and Back

Build and operate a drone/glider capable of reaching as high as possible, while measuring atmospheric state variables and land safely.

Read more & join the challenge

EXPERIMENTAL MISSION: Extreme Conditions

How long can a drone operate effectively when exposed to wintertime in-cloud icing conditions? The mission will be executed in a VTT cold lab.

Read more & join the challenge
